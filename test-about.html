About Me

Hi, I'm Collier Campbell. I'm a skilled UX designer and human factors engineer who specializes in creating user-friendly and visually aesthetic designs. I have a background in human factors and UX design with over 5 years of experience tackling challenges facing users. I strive to create experiences that are both intuitive and engaging. My work is focused on collecting and understanding user needs and transforming them into effective, easy-to-understand design solutions.


In addition to my professional work in human factors and UX design, I am exploring computer programming and artificial intelligence as an outside challenge. In fact, this website represents my first real attempt at using HTML and CSS to create a functioning user interface via programming. Along with the help of AI, my website was created and iterated on over the course of many weeks of trial and error.


Outside of my professional life, I enjoy the outdoors, staying active, and pursuing new hobbies. Those hobbies include but are not limited to cooking, fitness, woodworking, and reading.
